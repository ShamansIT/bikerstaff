Company History

We have been developing layouts and souvenirs for over 12 years. During this time, we have grown from two private entrepreneurs who did all the work with their own hands, into a full-cycle manufacturing company.

In our own production, laser cutting and milling services are provided according to customer samples. Manufactured products delivered throughout Ukraine and Europe.

Mission & Vission

Because of first-class creative ideas, we help art to become a part of your life. Modern methods, close cooperation and five-star level of work with customers help us to grow business and improve quality.
